Welcome to StatusLex!

StatusLex allows citizens to stay up-to-date with the status of Code Enforcement Cases and Building Permits in Lexington, KY.

Currently, StatusLex displays additional information for CityGram notifications, but future improvements will include the ability to search for cases and permits, as well as additional datasets.
